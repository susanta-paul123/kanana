Kenana food products

From field to plate: we transform crops into sustainable food and energy sources that meet the needs of agribusinesses and consumers

We are on a mission to leverage agricultural raw materials to the fullest extent while offering sustainable solutions that address emerging nutritional challenges without compromising on quality and quantity.

Since our inception, we have successfully capitalised on sugarcane's extensive applications and explored other areas of sustainable food and energy production practices.

We now produce a wide range of products such as environmental friendly biofuels, animal feed, milk and dairy products, meat and poultry, wooden products from commercial forests, horticultural products, and biocompost in addition to other engineering goods.

Kenana sugar and sugarcane by-products

Explore our extensive cane growing and sugar manufacturing operation

-Sugar represents our company’s core business. We yielded our first sugarcane crop in 1980 and reached design capacity in the first five years of operation. Currently, 90% of our sugar produce is sold domestically and 10% is exported.

-Our annual plant output is 100,000 tons of sugar, which represents 60% of the total production and consumption in the domestic market

-Our corporate cane sugar farm is spread over 100,000 acres with an average yield per acre of 47 MT, the highest in the world

-Our production capacity at commissioning is 17,000 MT of cane per day to produce 300,000 MT

At present we produce:

  • 5 types of sugar
  • Special Green Cane
  • Types of Cane Syrup; Amber and Treacle

Our by-products include:

  • Molasses: 120,000
  • Filter Mud: 150,000 tons
  • Bagasse: 1.25 million
  • Green tops: 100,000 tons
  • Cogeneration: 75 MW
  • Steam

Kenana Animal Feed

Established in 2004, our animal feed project aims to produce a complete, balanced, and sustainable animal feed solution for the livestock industry. Our main ingredients are the sugar by-products, molasses and bagasse. Our plant, which boasts a 100,000 MT per year production capacity, is high-tech and fully computerised to ensure consistent feed quality. It's also a highly flexible facility that can cater to customised specifications. Currently, 80% of our feed is sold domestically, while 20% is exported.

Our animal feed products include:

  • Fattening feed.
  • Milking feed.
  • Three types of poultry feed (starter, finisher, and layer)
  • Green fodder
  • Concentrate

Animal feed specifications:

  • Ingredients: Sorghum, oil cake, molasses, bagasse, wheat bran, limestone, urea and salt
  • Product form: 8 mm pellets
  • Type of Feed: complete diets for fattening cows and sheep (concentrate and roughage) with balanced energy and proteins and necessary pre-mixes
  • Packed in 25-50 kg green polypropylene bags

Calculated chemical composition (% of dry matter):

  • Dry matter: 90.0 (minimum)
  • Crude protein: 17.9
  • Crude fiber: 11.9
  • Ash: 11.2
  • Metabolisable energy: 10.5 MJ/kg OM

Kenana poultry, meat, and dairy

Poultry

Our poultry business was established in 2007 and features closed houses with a capacity of approximately 12,500 chicks per house. To improve efficiency, we recently launched a new slaughterhouse with a 0.840 million kg of chicken meat per year production capacity. We also offer table eggs and frozen chicken products, with a poultry expansion project underway to increase production.

  • Operating model: closed houses with a capacity of 12,500 chicks per house
  • Slaughterhouse: capacity to produce 1800 chickens per hour and operate up to 270 days per year
  • Market: all production sold to wholesalers in the local market
  • Production capacity: approximately 0.840 million kg of chicken meat per year
  • Products: table eggs and frozen chicken
  • Poultry expansion project: to produce 2500 MT of frozen chicken, 53 million table eggs in phase 2 and a hatchery to produce 1.5 million chicks

Meat products

The fattening and meat project is another aspect of our business which includes a slaughterhouse for bulls and sheep. This project comprises a 35,000 breeding herd and 25,000 bulls for fattening in order to produce 18 million kg of beef.
