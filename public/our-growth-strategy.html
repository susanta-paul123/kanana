Our growth strategy

Leveraging our reputation and expertise for growth and development

Our legacy and strong brand position makes us exceedingly credible among all our stakeholders, from shareholders to customers, suppliers, employees, and vendors. We therefore believe that now is the right time to capitalise on our current reputation by expanding our business through investments. By doing so, we aim to stimulate our brand's future and sustain its unique competitive position in both regional and global markets.

Over the years, we have pursued a visionary expansion strategy

By investing in our brand’s strength and status, and establishing Kenana's traditions of providing natural products in harmony with the environment, we have seen results in revenue maximisation. We have successfully achieved most of our planned marketing goals and secured our path as leaders in the domestic and global sugar industry with sights on achieving food security in this strategic commodity.

Cementing our position as trailblazers in the renewable fuel industry

We have diversified into related industries, evolving beyond sugar to enter the world of renewable green fuel. This move has also prepared us to comply with international trade rules and integrated quality management systems. Through these efforts, we have demonstrated our commitment to sustainable practices and cemented our reputation as a valuable and trusted brand.
