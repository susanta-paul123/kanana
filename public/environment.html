Our commitment to The Environment

KSCs innovation model

We are dedicated to developing the Sudanese economy through creative and innovative approaches that aim to achieve zero waste while creating green economies and reducing carbon emissions. As an innovation model, KSC has developed responsible business practices that promote the protection and preservation of the environment.

Green Energy Projects

Our green energy projects are directly linked to our Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) objectives:

  • We promote renewables not only in our production facilities but also in the community buildings that have been constructed around the estate
  • We use water efficiently for irrigation and factory processing and generate power from cane by-products such as bagasse
  • KSC also produces ethanol fuel from molasses and creates second-generation ethanol from cellulose from sugarcane waste
  • The company recycles water from the factory for irrigation, harvests water for supplementary irrigation, and produces animal feed from sugar tops, molasses, and bagasse
  • We produce bio-fertilizers from vinasse, filter mud, waste products, and animal manure
  • And we generate solar energy, not only for its land but also for the small and remote villages surrounding KSC

The Benefits of Sustainable Business

Our sustainable model also benefits the country as a whole, especially since agriculture is such an important sector of the economy. As the largest sugar corporation in Sudan, KSC provides much-needed tax revenues and jobs.

Our Strategy to Reduce Carbon Emissions

Our goal is to downscale overall energy use, minimize the use of raw materials, and reduce waste.

Actions we're taking to reduce our carbon footprint:

  • Improving ethanol production from molasses and sweet potato, cogenerating and exporting as much power as possible
  • Maximizing cane yield and factory recovery
  • Generating biogas from wastes such as vinasse by anaerobic processes
  • Reducing fertilizer and chemical inputs
  • Reducing cane burning to zero
  • Reducing supplementary fuels by introducing solar energy
  • Minimizing irrigation power input
  • Reducing cane transport distances
  • Recycling water to reduce water intake

Our Environmental Policy and Objectives

We manage our processes, materials and people to reduce and minimize the negative environmental impacts associated with our business goals. The main objectives are to investigate the possibility of reducing hazardous and toxic materials, reduce, reuse, and recycle wastes and improve the efficiency of energy use.

Join us on this journey to build a better world for everyone.
