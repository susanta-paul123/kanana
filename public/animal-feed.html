Kenana animal feed

Providing quality animal feed for complete nutrition and thriving livestock

Established in 2004, our animal feed project aims to produce a complete, balanced, and sustainable animal feed solution for the livestock industry. Our main ingredients are the sugar by-products, molasses and bagasse. Our plant, which boasts a 100,000 MT per year production capacity, is high-tech and fully computerised to ensure consistent feed quality. It's also a highly flexible facility that can cater to customised specifications. Currently, 80% of our feed is sold domestically, while 20% is exported.
