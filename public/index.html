Driving sustainable growth as one of the world's largest sugar-producing companies

At Kenana, we're committed to achieving food security through our diverse solutions spanning sugar production and additional agro-industrial projects.

Ensuring sustainable food security and socio-economic business development in the Middle East and beyond

Kenana Sugar Company Ltd. is a leading manufacturer of refined sugar and its by-product

Established in 1975 as a sugar-producing company, Kenana Sugar Company Ltd. has diversified its offering to include a host of additional solutions that extend beyond refined sugar. We combine an innovative approach with industry-leading technologies to provide solutions spanning animal feed, dairy products, biofuel, compost, meat, poultry and horticulture, to name a few.

Delivering greener sources of food and renewable energy

At the heart of Kenana lies our commitment to the production of environmentally-friendly food and products led by innovative research and development. Our solutions include:

Sugarcane and refined sugar production

Biofuel (ethanol) production

Human and animal food production

Compost and horticulture production

Engineering and sustainable agribusiness solutions

Project delivery and technical expertise

Our passion for growth has led to a number of strategically commissioned subsidiaries

Kenana has been fortunate to enjoy rapid expansion over its many years in the industry

Since our establishment in 1975, we have founded a number of highly successful companies to further our business development while continuing to meet the ever-changing needs of the local and international markets.

Our subsidiaries include Kenana Equipment & Machinery, Kenana Engineering & Technical Services, Kenana Animal Feed, and Kenana Poultry.
