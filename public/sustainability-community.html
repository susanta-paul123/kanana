Setting the standard for agro-processing in rural settings

We believe in the power of community and the importance of sustainability. That's why we're helping to develop the surrounding villages and people to thrive both socially and economically. We work hard to help them improve their quality of living by providing better housing, affordable education, high-standard medical facilities, clean drinking water, accessible roads, employment opportunities and recreational activities.

Our initial investment in social development has transformed previously barren land into a modern living community. We take our responsibility seriously and are dedicated to making a difference in people's lives.

How we're making a difference in our community:

Social Development

We have made tremendous changes in both rural and urban areas. We are promoting renewable energy, not only in our production facilities but in our community buildings. At present, we have constructed around 58 schools and learning centers on the KSC site which serve over 12,000 primary and secondary students. We have also built eight libraries and sport facilities for the community.

Employment

We provide employment opportunities to over 5,258 full-time and 6,389 part-time employees. In addition, over 30,000 people live and work on the KSC estate, which contributes to the overall growth and development of the surrounding area.

Partnerships

We believe in the power of partnerships and working together to achieve common goals. That's why we extend our role in different outside communities, such as education. We have partnered with the British Embassy to sponsor the Chevening program since 1995, and we have also sponsored three seats annually with a total of 75,000 pound sterling. In 2016, we suggested that one of the three seats be devoted to energy studies. And in 2017, a scholar went to the University of Manchester to study advanced process design for energy.

Cultural Programmes

At Kenana, we're not just about building infrastructure and creating jobs. We also sponsor cultural programmes that support the youth and community in addition to the health and awareness programs. We believe in giving back to the community in meaningful ways and making a positive impact on people's lives.
