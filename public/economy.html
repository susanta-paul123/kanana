Our commitment to The Economy

Helping Sudan’s growth and development

Our commitment to sustainability goes hand-in-hand with our commitment to the economy. We recognize our responsibility to provide much-needed tax revenues and jobs. But we also strive to be a model for a win-win economic approach that benefits both the people and the environment.

How we're making a difference in the economy:

Infrastructure: Supporting villages with affordable services

We’ve developed a site with agricultural villages that offer full affordable services. These include modern housing, educational facilities, medical facilities, recreational activities, and accessible roads.

Water Treatment: Providing clean drinking water

Clean drinking water is a human right. We’re proud to provide 4.4 million cubic meters of drinking water to the communities surrounding KSC.

Energy: Providing reliable, sustainable power

Access to reliable, sustainable power is essential for any growing economy. That’s why we provide a regular supply of power, generating 3.5 MW through renewable sources.

Healthcare: Accessible and high-quality

Everyone deserves access to good healthcare. That’s why we’ve built one hospital and 12 health centers and clinics, serving more than 140,000 patients annually from our project and the surrounding areas.
