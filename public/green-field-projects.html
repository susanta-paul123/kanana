Greenfield Projects

As a company committed to growth and innovation, we are always looking for opportunities to expand our reach and develop new ventures. Our greenfield projects allow us to start from scratch and build new facilities and operations that are designed to meet the needs of today's market. By leveraging our expertise in research, engineering, and project management, we are able to create efficient and effective solutions that deliver results for our clients. Our focus on sustainability and environmental responsibility means that we are able to develop projects that meet the highest standards for efficiency and impact, while also creating long-term value for our stakeholders.

Ethanol Expansion Project

Key objectives:

  • Increase ethanol production to 60 million liters
  • Increase supply of feedstock by planting sweet potato
  • Offer mechanical harvesting

Poultry Expansion Project

Key objectives:

  • 2500 MT of frozen chicken
  • 53 million table eggs in phase 2
  • 1.5 million chicks from the hatchery
  • Create biogas via recycling waste

Biogas Project

Key objectives:

  • Clean energy compliance with environmental regulations by reducing of GHG
  • Production of 92000 CBM per day
  • Zero waste-management recycling system

Organic Fertilizer Project

Key objectives:

  • 2500 MT of frozen chicken
  • 53 million table eggs in phase 2
  • 1.5 million chicks from the hatchery
  • Create biogas via recycling waste

Fruit Farm Horticulture Project

Key objectives:

  • Cultivating 1000 acres of farmland to produce bananas
  • Cultivating of 400 acres of farmland to produce mangos
  • Recycling of the harvesting residuals to produce organic fertilizers
