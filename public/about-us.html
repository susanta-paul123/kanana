About Kenana

Founded in 1975, Kenana Sugar Company Ltd. is one of the world's largest sugar-producing companies

Kenana is a leading manufacturer of refined cane sugar and its by-products. Our factory is located on the banks of the White Nile to the southeast of Kosti city, with the Kenana estate covering an expansive 100,000 acres of irrigated land and clay plains.

Over time, we have diversified our offering to include refined sugar, animal and human food, biofuels like ethanol, wooden products from commercial forestry, certified seeds, and a host of engineering products and services.

Strategic growth

At Kenana, we aspire to grow as an international industry leader through the supply of commodities and renewable energy solutions

Kenana Sugar Company Ltd. is committed to solidifying our name on the local and international markets while ensuring resilience as a business. With a leading reputation and significant investment from stakeholders, we endeavour to continue building strong business partnerships with leading public and private organisations.

We're dedicated to continue supplying our diverse range of products and services at the most competitive rates without compromising on consistency, quality, and safety.

Renewable energy solutions

Ensuring greener sources of heat and energy generation is an integral part of our operations

With sugar cane being our primary product and main activity within the business, we have enabled the cogeneration of electricity for cane production using by-products of sugarcane, namely molasses and baggase, to produce heat and energy.

By leveraging these by-products, we are able to drive cogeneration, using surplus baggase to produce heat and power. Cogeneration is both an energy-efficient and environmentally beneficial solution to power our sugar mill. Additionally, it ensures self-sufficiency and lowers our dependence on the national government for investment into power generation.
