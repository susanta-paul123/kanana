Our subsidiaries

Integrated agricultural solutions spanning the entire agribusiness value chain

We have strategically commissioned several subsidiaries as a result of our passion for growth and have been fortunate to experience rapid expansion over our many years in the industry. Today, we offer a broad range of project consulting and engineering services, including business development and project management.

Kenana Engineering & Technical Services (KETS)

A leading provider of agro-industrial solutions in Sudan and neighboring African countries since 1986

KETS was established due to the immense demand for affordable consulting and engineering services in Sudan’s Agro-industrial sector, especially within the country’s prominent sugar industry. Our determination, coupled with our highly qualified and dedicated group of staff has resulted in excellence as we strive to become a leader in our field.

We provide a broad range of services, including:
  • Business development
  • Engineering and project management
  • Consulting and contracting
  • Organisational planning and capacity building
  • Capacity building
  • Water treatment and management
  • Legal services
