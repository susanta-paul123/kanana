Our business beyond sugar

Kenana's bio-enterprise portfolio: driving value and sustainability through agricultural progress

Sugarcane cultivation is our core business, but we have evolved to offer more than just sugar products. Our unique and diverse business model combines multiple value-added agro-industrial projects bolstered by years of technical expertise. These projects are managed cyclically to optimise collaboration and turn our vision of a renewable source of food and energy into a reality, while keeping the zero-waste goal top of mind.

We aim to become a worldwide agro-industrial conglomerate that will continue to exceed expectations in our mission to produce environmentally friendly goods and services, now and in the future.

Explore our diverse portfolio to learn more about how we are contributing to a sustainable future.

Bio Gas

Research & Development (R&D) at Kenana

Remaining steadfast in our dedication to continuous improvement

In our efforts to enhance the quality and yield of sugarcane and its by-products, we embarked on a comprehensive R&D programme which was fundamental to improving product quality, meeting local and international demands, and establishing new business divisions.

Our R&D projects cover the following areas:
  • Weed control and growth regulators
  • Crop protection (entomology and pathology)
  • Sugarcane breeding
  • Agriculture engineering (irrigation)
  • Industrial research (sugar technology, ethanol, and biogas)
  • Horticulture research (tissue culture, fruits, and vegetables
  • Animal production (animal nutrition and animal health)
