Kenana Energies

We are dedicated to building a thrivinng bioeconomy through efficient and environmentally friendly energy solutions

We built Sudan's first Bioethanol plant in 2009 and are dedicated to promoting sustainable practices and producing renewable energy that benefits both the environment and the economy. To achieve this, we have implemented several bioproduct initiatives aimed at creating cleaner and more efficient sources of energy.

Our bioproducts include bioethanol, biogas, and biocompost, each with its own unique benefits.

Bioethanol

A renewable and sustainable fuel alternative

Bioethanol is a liquid biofuel alternative to conventional fossil fuels that is mainly produced by feedstock yeast fermentation. Our superior anhydrous ethanol is a high-value product with enormous export potential.

Bioethanol product details and production metrics:

  • By-products: yeast, second grade alcohol, vinasse, and CO2

  • The plant produces 200,000 liters per day and 65,000,000 ml per year

  • 90% or our bioethanol is exported and 10% is sold domestically

Why Bioethanol ?

-Increases national income

-New source of energy supply

-Reduces dependency on oil and its fluctuating prices

-Promotion of new revenue streams

Environmental Benefits:

-Reduces harmful tailpipe emissions that cause global warming

-Offers the optimum allocation of sugar by-products

Biogas

We are dedicated to building a thrivinng bioeconomy through efficient and environmentally friendly energy solutions

Biogas is a sustainable and eco-friendly energy source produced through anaerobic digestion of organic matter. It is highly efficient in converting high-energy waste streams into fuel, thereby preventing them from ending up in landfills. Additionally, the process generates useful by-products that act as a fiscal multiplier. By turning waste into a valuable resource, biogas promotes a circular economy, while also reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and odours derived from waste.

Biogas product details and production metrics:

  • By-product: treated vinasse
  • 95,000 m3 per day
  • 28,500,000 m3 annually

Why Biogas

Environmental benefits:

Captures and uses methane, a greenhouse gas that is 21 times more potent than CO2

Converts high-energy waste streams into fuel, diverting them from landfills

Helps to remove odour-causing compounds

Economic benefits:

The production of biogas results in the creation of valuable by-products from waste materials

It can also lead to local job creation and economic development

Cogeneration

Efficient and sustainable energy production

Cogeneration as an integral part of our business. Also known as combined heat and power (CHP), it offers several advantages such as increased energy efficiency, reduced greenhouse gas emissions, and cost savings by utilising waste heat for heating, cooling or power generation. It's therefore a more sustainable and economical energy solution than traditional power generation systems. We integrate cogeneration into our operations by utilizing bagasse for energy production. Both molasses and bagasse are used to generate energy.

  • Electricity used for cane processing/powerhouse
  • Production: 75 megawatts
